Expert Geotechnical & NDT Services

At Elite NDT & Geotech Engineering, we understand that every successful construction project begins with a thorough understanding of the ground beneath. Our comprehensive geotechnical investigation and non-destructive testing services provide critical insights that ensure structural integrity, safety compliance, and optimal design parameters.

With state-of-the-art equipment and a team of certified engineers, we deliver precise data and actionable recommendations that help prevent costly structural issues, reduce construction risks, and extend infrastructure lifespan.

Our Specialized Services

Comprehensive testing and analysis solutions to ensure your project's foundation is built on solid ground.

Soil Bearing Capacity Test

Determine the safe bearing capacity of soil to support foundations and prevent structural settlement issues.

Plate Load Test

Evaluate soil deformation characteristics and load-bearing capacity through direct field testing methods.

Core Cutting & Rebound Hammer Test

Assess concrete strength and quality through non-destructive and minimally invasive testing methods.

Pile Integrity Testing

Verify structural integrity of deep foundations using sonic echo and impulse response methods.

Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity Test

Detect internal flaws, cracks, and voids in concrete structures using advanced ultrasonic technology.

Detailed Site Report & Recommendations

Comprehensive analysis and expert recommendations based on test results to optimize your project design.
